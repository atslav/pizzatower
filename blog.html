There are 2 playable characters in total Peppino and Gustavo. Well... it's three with the rat, BUT there were plans for having more characters. Peppino Spaghetti

He is a chef that owns a pizza place in somewhere, where? Nobody knows! And he is also in debt hooray! He also delivered pizza to a famous video game character! Snick the porcupine in sage 2019. His character is focused on speed and SPEED!

Gustavo and Brick the rat

He is a sous-chef gnome that works with peppino! And he also has a pet rat called Brick the rat! Really cool! First time when you see them in game Gustavo runs in circles away from Brick. On the next floor they start fighting and then on the other floor they settle their differences and become buddies.
